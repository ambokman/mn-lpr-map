Frequently Asked Questions

What is a license plate reader (LPR)?

A camera system that uses optical character recognition to automatically read vehicle license plates and timestamp vehicle locations.

Are LPRs legal in Minnesota?

Yes. Minnesota law regulates LPR data retention and reporting.

Is this data complete?

No. This map shows only known and documented LPR locations. Some are approximate.
